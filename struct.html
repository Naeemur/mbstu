Infrastructure

Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University campus consists of two main academic buildings, administrative building, central library, central mosque, library and cafeteria, bank and medical center. The medical centre of MBSTU is equipped with necessary instruments and doctor to provide appropriate health care to the students and it is always ready to face any sudden situation.

Shahzaman Deeghi, Zia park adorns the beauty and calmness of the campus.

Mazar-i-Sharif of nMawlana Abdul Hamid Khan Bhashani and Darbar Hall holds the memories of Mawlana Bhashani.

To pay homage to the martyrs of Bangladesh there are Shahid Minar, Prottoy Ekattor and a monument on liberation war.

The university campus has a large play ground where different sport competitions take place. The Physical Education Department plays a significant role in the campus. It occasionally organizes different departmental sport events to cheer the students up. It also maintains & organizes the annual sports of MBSTU. This department also runs the gymnasium to facilitate the students to keep sound health.

MBSTU leads a lifestyle which is colorful, exciting & full of various interesting events. The students are ought to attend their classes from 8:00a.m to 5:00 p.m during the weekdays - Saturday to Wednesday. Thursday and Friday are weekend. The students get the opportunity to visit various interesting places as educational tours every now and then.

The students are inhabited in five distinctive halls. Muktijodda Abdul Mannan Hall, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Hall and Shahid Ziaur Rahman Hall are for the male students & Alema Khatun Bhashani Hall and Shahid Janoni Jahanara Imam Hall are for the female students. The halls are multistoried with all kinds of modern facilities.

Various cultural activities such as Ekushe February, Sadhinota Dibosh, Bijoy Dibosh, Nobo Borsho, Pohela Falgun & Boshonto Boron are celebrated with proper respect & ritual regarding to the occasion. Besides these national annual events, many more activities such as programming contests, seminars on environmental crisis, debating, film festivals etc. are organized throughout the whole year.

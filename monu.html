Monuments

To pay homage to the martyrs Language movement and Liberation War of Bangladesh there are Shahid Minar, Prottoy Ekattor and a monument on liberation war.

The sculpture “Prottoy Ekattor” is made by Sculptor Mrinal Haque in the memories of valiant fight for the liberation of Bangladesh.

Muktamancha is made to respect two legends of Bangladesh national poet Kazi Nazrul Islam and Rabindranath Tagore.

Ekushe February, Sadhinota Dibosh, Bijoy Dibosh are celebrated with paying proper respect to these monuments.
